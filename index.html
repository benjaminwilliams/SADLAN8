What is SADLAN?

SADLAN is a LAN party originally created as an alternative to Schoolies after a group od friends graduated from high school. Now in its 8th year, SADLAN has grown from school chums fumbling about with networking a couple of computers together to an Australia wide institution.

SADLAN has a long and proud tradition. While the first official SADLAN did not take place until 2006, unofficial "underground" LAN's can be traced back to 2003.

Traditionally, SADLAN has taken place in OSIC's garage. SADLAN 7 was held in Melbourne and was a great success. This year SADLAN returns to the Central Coast NSW for the 8th installment.
